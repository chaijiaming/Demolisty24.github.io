The Portfolio

In this portfolio, I placed the focus of readability and ease of navigation as the core of the website. I believe that a portfolio with a very smooth navigation and readability is very important to a reader who might not know how to start. Also, I have attempt to condense each page of the portfolio to make each page too clustered to greatly improve readability. The navigation bar is fixed at the top to allow for full viewing experience if the page is long and requires scrolling. In each page, I have attempted to navigation the reader to another page in the text as they are reading to allow for a smooth navigation of the website. I have also considered to reduce the amount of technical jargon that is used in this site to allow for various visitors/readers to have a easier time reading the description behind my projects.
