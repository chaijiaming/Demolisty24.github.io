AlexaMD

AlexaMD is an Amazon Alexa command that provides instant medical diagnosis at home created at HackDartmouthIII hosted by Dartmouth College. This idea was inspiried by the rising cost of medical bill that many American are currently face, those with a lower insurance coverage can see a medical bill range from 80 - 200 USD from single doctor visit. This project is meant to provide medical diagonsis for more common and minicule conditions and also somemore serious conditions that is not only accurate but also affordable, however, it is not meant to replace the physician but just a affordable alternative.

Alexa++

Alexa++ is an Amazon Alexa command adds two different functions to your Amazon Echo/Dot. This project revolves around 2 of our daily focuses, food and socializing. Alexa++ is divided into 2 function, Chef Alexa++ which provides the user a step by step instruction to the dish you want to make and recommended dishes that is similar to it. The other function, called Feels, will allow the user to see how others people feel aboutcurrent trending and releveant news that you care about analysised based on how other react with emojis on twitter.
